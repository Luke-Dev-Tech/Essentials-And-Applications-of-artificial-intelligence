How To Use Recycling AI

Follow these simple steps to classify your waste using AI.

Step 1

Open the Homepage

Navigate to the homepage of the Recycling AI website. You will see the upload section where you can drag and drop your image or click to browse a file from your device.

Step 2

Upload Your Trash Image

Drag and drop an image into the upload area or click the browse button to select an image file. Ensure the file size does not exceed 10MB.

Step 3

Preview the Image

After selecting your file, the image preview will appear. Verify that the uploaded image is clear and correctly selected before proceeding.

Step 4

Click Predict

Press the Predict button to send the image to the AI model. A scanning animation and loading spinner will appear while the system analyzes the image.

Step 5

View Prediction Result

The AI will display the predicted waste category along with a confidence percentage. The progress bar indicates how confident the model is in its classification.
